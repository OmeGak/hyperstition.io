The Work

A collective oracle that discovers fictions capable of becoming real

An interactive installation using the structure of a 20-questions game to perform a collective act of speculation. Each yes/no answer creates a space of possible futures. What emerges is a hyperstition - a future that begins to feel actionable - along with concrete rituals for bringing it into the present.

Process
01
The participant asks. Yes/no questions to the oracle. The AI maintains consistency, letting a pattern crystallize without knowing what it will be.
02
The oracle asks. In reverse mode, the oracle interrogates the participant — scattering across technology, bodies, ecology, the sacred, the mundane.
03
The hyperstition emerges. The system interprets the pattern, names a concept, and describes what changes if people begin acting as though it were true.
04
Actions are given. Concrete acts — rituals disguised as to-do items — that bridge the fictional and the real.
