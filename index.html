01 — THE CONCEPT

"Fictions that make themselves real."

A deliberate methodology for envisioning and enacting radical futures.

Hyperstitions are fictions that make themselves real: ideas, narratives, or cultural artefacts that —by virtue of being believed and acted upon— generate the very conditions for their own realization.

02

02 — ABDUCTION

"The very existence of an idea generates the activity and resources that bring it into being."

Hyperstition is the deliberate construction of a future so vivid and compelling that it exerts a gravitational pull on present-day actions, resources, and imagination. It emerges from radical imagination unconstrained by present limitations and free from any path dependencies.

Once a narrative gets encoded into media, markets, or technical systems, the hyperstition becomes structurally embedded, and no longer depends on any single person believing it.

Hyperstition orients without fixing a destination: the vision pulls toward a direction even as that destination remains in the making.

03

03 — DELIBERATE ACT

"Hyperstition is a form of magic."

An incantation capable of bringing about futures that would not be conceivable from purely deductive logic — using the force of narrative, collective belief, and coordinated action to shift the boundary between what is and what could be.

This is a collective effort: people who share similar values coming together to conceive the narratives that will, in turn, enlist them in bringing those narratives to life. It is not the future that imposes itself upon us — it is us, as a collective, who empower ourselves by constructing compelling narratives that connect past, present, and future in new configurations.

"Hyperstitioning" is both a creative and political act: it retroactively reshapes the interpretation of the past, restructures the priorities of the present, and opens a pathway toward a future that did not previously exist. It is not just something to observe but a practice to engage deliberately.

Craft the fictions that ought to become real. Engineer the retro-causal loop that brings them into being.
